TV Series 2024
☆☆☆☆☆ 16+
Dandadan is a supernatural and comedic manga that centers on two teenagers, Takakura, an occult enthusiast who doesn't believe in ghosts, and Ayase, a girl who is skeptical about aliens. The two make a bet to prove each other's beliefs wrong, but their lives take a turn when they discover that both ghosts and extraterrestrial beings are real. This revelation sets them off on a wild adventure filled with bizarre encounters, battles with otherworldly creatures, and a growing romance. The story blends sci-fi and the paranormal with humor and emotional depth, all while exploring themes of friendship and love amidst chaotic, larger-than-life events.
